Burger Recipe

Description

A burger is a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty (usually beef, chicken, or veggie) placed inside a sliced bun, often with lettuce, tomato, cheese, sauces, and pickles.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup and mustard

Steps

  1. Mix ground meat with salt, pepper, and spices of choice.
  2. Form the mixture into patties, about 1 inch thick.
  3. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.
  5. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  6. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  7. Assemble the burger by placing the patty on the bottom bun.
  8. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and any sauces you like.
  9. Top with the other half of the bun.
