Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a round, flat base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings like vegetables, meat, or herbs, then baked in an oven.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese (mozzarella or your choice)
  • Toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Steps

  1. Make the dough by mixing flour, yeast, water, salt, sugar, and oil.
  2. Knead the dough until smooth, then let it rise for about 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  5. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  6. Sprinkle cheese generously over the sauce.
  7. Add your favorite toppings.
  8. Transfer the pizza to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
