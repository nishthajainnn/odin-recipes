Lasagna recipe

Description

There are just three components that make a lasagna great: the pasta, the cheese, and the sauce. Since this recipe starts with a jar of marinara for ease, make sure it's one that tastes great. My favorite brand is Rao's, but choose your personal favorite. The lasagna noodles cook right in the sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • meat sauce
  • ricotta cheese
  • onions
  • mozzarella cheese
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, sauté onions and garlic until translucent.
  4. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with herbs and spices.
  7. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  8. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella on top.
  9. Bake for 30-40 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
